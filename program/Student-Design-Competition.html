ISCAS Student Design Competition

Event Overview

The ISCAS Student Design Competition provides student teams with an opportunity to present innovative circuit and system designs and compete at the world level during ISCAS 2026.

  • Details: https://ieee-cas.org/society-involvement/student-design-competition
  • Final Competition Time: 15:00~16:00, 2026-May-25th
  • Final Competition Venue: River Hall, Shanghai ICC (Conference Venue)
