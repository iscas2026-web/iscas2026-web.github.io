Official Partner Hotel

Shanghai Oriental Riverside Hotel (International Convention Center)

No. 2727 Binjiang Avenue, Pudong New Area, Shanghai 200120

100m from venue, 2 min walk

Special Conference Rate: Market price with complimentary breakfast included!

The Shanghai Oriental Riverside Hotel features spacious rooms with stunning panoramic views of the Huangpu River. The hotel offers various dining options including Cantonese and Huaiyang cuisine, as well as elegant Western restaurants. The lobby bar and rooftop bar provide perfect relaxation spots. The hotel boasts a lecture hall for 1,000+ guests, a grand banquet hall for 4,000+ guests, and 21 meeting rooms accommodating 50-300 people.
