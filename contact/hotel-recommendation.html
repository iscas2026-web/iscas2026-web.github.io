Hotel Recommendation

Jing An Shangri-La, Shanghai (上海静安香格里拉大酒店)

Price: 1000+ RMB

Located in the Jing An Kerry Centre on West Yan'an Road, Jing An Shangri-La is adjacent to the Shanghai Exhibition Centre and just steps away from the Jing An Temple and the shopping paradise of Nanjing Road.

The hotel features guestrooms ranging from 43 to 311 sqm. The Horizon Club on the 55th floor offers express check-in/out, buffet breakfast, and cocktails for guests in executive rooms and suites.

Dining options include the 1515 West Chophouse & Bar, Summer Palace (Chinese cuisine), and more. Both 1515 West Chophouse & Bar and Summer Palace were listed in the Michelin Guide Shanghai 2017.

UrCove by HYATT Shanghai Jing'an (上海静安逸扉酒店)

Price: 400+ RMB

Located in the bustling Jing'an Temple business district, this UrCove hotel offers an all-day restaurant, 24-hour gym, meeting rooms, and comfortable guestrooms. You can enjoy city views through the floor-to-ceiling windows from the spiral staircase in the lobby.

The hotel is right in front of Exit 2 of Wuding Road Station on Metro Line 14, providing easy access to all parts of the city. It is surrounded by shopping malls, high-end office buildings, and schools. Guests can also walk to historic villas and garden streets to experience the charm of Shanghai under the plane trees.

Rugui Hotel (筎归酒店)

Price: 100+ RMB

Adjacent to the Global Harbor shopping center with extremely convenient transportation. It is a 2-minute walk to Jinshajiang Road Station (Metro Lines 3, 4, and 13). A 21-minute metro ride takes you directly to Nanjing East Road Pedestrian Street, People's Square, the Bund, and Yu Garden. It is about a 30-minute drive to Hongqiao Airport/Railway Station.

Rooms are equipped with smart systems, premium customized fragrances, and comfortable bedding.
