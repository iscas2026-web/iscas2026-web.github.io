About Shanghai ICC

Located atop Shanghai Metro South Shaanxi Road station, Shanghai ICC is on Middle Huaihai Road in the busy commercial district of Puxi. Local Transportation

Shanghai has a comprehensive public transportation network including metro, bus, and taxi services. The venue is conveniently located at South Shaanxi Road Station, where Metro Lines 1, 10 and 12 intersect. For detailed transportation information and travel tips in Shanghai, please refer to this guide.

Accommodation

The IAPM mall at Shanghai ICC covers 1.3 million square feet and features top international retail flagship brands, an IMAX cinema, and a diverse food zone offering local and international cuisine. The mall is specially designed for late-night shopping with extended hours from 10:00 am to 11:00 pm, while restaurants remain open until midnight. For more information, visit IAPM Mall.
Shanghai ICC is surrounded by various high-quality hotels within walking distance. The area around Huaihai Road is home to numerous international hotel chains and boutique hotels. For more accommodation options and special conference rates, please visit Shanghai Hotels Guide.
