Conference Location

Shanghai International Convention Center

IEEE ISCAS 2026 will be held at the Shanghai International Convention Center, a landmark waterfront venue in the Lujiazui area of Pudong. Located on Binjiang Avenue along the Huangpu River, the venue offers a distinctive conference setting with immediate access to Shanghai's most recognizable skyline.

The convention center sits next to the Oriental Pearl Tower and is close to the Shanghai Tower, Shanghai World Financial Center, and Jin Mao Tower. This makes it convenient for attendees to combine conference activities with city sightseeing, dining, and evening walks along the riverfront.

Address

No. 2727 Binjiang Avenue, Pudong New Area, Shanghai 200120, China.

Public Transportation

The venue is conveniently accessible from Lujiazui Station on Shanghai Metro Line 2.

Venue Profile

A major conference venue in Pudong with large event space, meeting facilities, and adjacent hotel support.

Nearby Landmarks

Oriental Pearl Tower, the Bund river view, Shanghai Tower, and the Lujiazui financial district.
