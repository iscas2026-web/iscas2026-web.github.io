IEEE International Symposium on Circuits and Systems (ISCAS) is the flagship conference of the IEEE Circuits and Systems Society (CASS). This global annual event is one of the world's premiere conferences with a history of 59 Years. ISCAS typically attracts 1,000+ attendees from academia, research & industry spread across the globe in the fields of theory, design, and implementation of Circuits & Systems.
ISCAS 2026 isinspired by the theme "CAS for Intelligent Society", aiming to emphasis the potential of CAS Society to contribute essential technology challenges for the fast-changing society. This is also perfectly aligned with development goal of China. ISCAS 2026 is an ideal platform for your organization to showcase your technical leadership to the global community thereby providing an opportunity for future business growth. It is also a prominent forum to attract the best of the diverse talent pool for your future expansion.
