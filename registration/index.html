Author Registration

  • Author Registration Deadline: 23:59:59, 9 Feb 2026 (CST).
  • Each Full (non-student) Registration is valid for up to TWO paper submissions.
    • Every paper accepted for presentation at the symposium must be associated with one Full Registration by an author or co-author.
    • The registration fee covers the first two papers. If an author wishes to register more than two papers under a single Full Registration, an Additional Paper Fee of USD 200 applies for each subsequent paper.
  • All regular paper submissions are limited to a maximum of 5 pages.

Tutorials

For ISCAS 2026, Tutorials are FREE OF CHARGE for all registered participants. However, to manage capacity and ensure a quality experience, the following policy applies:

  • Each participant can select a maximum of two tutorial sessions (one in the morning and one in the afternoon).
  • Please select your preferred sessions during the online registration process.
  • Participants will receive their tutorial admission tickets at the conference registration desk on-site.

Please refer to the Tutorial Overview for more details on the available sessions.

Conference Policies

  • Cancellation Policy: All refund/cancellation requests must be provided in writing to hutong@kailimice.cn and received by 19 Apr 2026. There will be an administrative fee of USD 200 deducted from each refund.
  • Substitution Policy: All requests to substitute another person on an existing registration must be submitted in writing to hutong@kailimice.cn no later than 19 Apr 2026.
